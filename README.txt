This is a Minecraft 1.20.1 Forge Mod, add a tool "Ore Finder" to find ores (or blocks).
It has different material variants, different durability and searching ability, and there are two searching methods.
You can tweak the ability of the tool in config, take a balance between its searching ability and capability of PC.
requires: Minecraft 1.20.1
          Forge >=47.4.0

这是一个Minecraft 1.20.1 Forge模组，加入了寻找矿物（或者方块）的工具“探矿器”。
它具有不同的材质变种，对应不同的耐久和搜索能力，并且具有两种搜寻方式。
可以通过配置，调节工具的能力，在功能性与电脑的计算能力之间取得平衡。
需求：Minecraft 1.20.1
     Forge >=47.4.0
